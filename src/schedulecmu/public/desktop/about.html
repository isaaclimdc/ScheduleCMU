What is ScheduleCMU?

ScheduleCMU is a schedule planning app designed for Carnegie Mellon students to plan their course schedule from the convenience of their computer or their smartphone.

With ScheduleCMU, we intend to bring about a drastic change in the way the CMU community creates and views academic schedules. Our app, created from scratch, allows CMU students to plan and view their academic schedules in a user-friendly manner.

The CourseEvents feature allow students to view and collaboratively add ad-hoc events related to the courses they are taking, such as office hours or review sessions. These events will be shown in the calendar of any student who has added the related course to their list.

How does it work?

We made a tool that works well on both mobile devices and desktop clients. It allows you to sign up with your Facebook account. Then to verify that you are a CMU student, our app sends a verification email to the user's andrew email. Clicking the link provided in the email directs the user to the schedules page where he or she can then create a schedule.

Various buttons on this page allow you to browse courses by number and department and to add a course events to a course that you are signed up for.
