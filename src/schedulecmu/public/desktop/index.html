CMU Scheduling, Reimagined.

Have your schedule with you wherever you are, desktop or mobile.

Ever felt like you can't leave your desk midway through planning your schedule? Well no longer. ScheduleCMU lets you start planning your schedule on your computer, and pick up anytime on your mobile device. It doesn't even matter if you're on iOS, Android, Windows, or other platforms - we are cross-platform. Now you'll never have to pull out your computer just to check where that sleep-inducing 8am lecture is.

Broadcast office hours, review sessions and other course events.

One of ScheduleCMU's unique features is that it allows you to add course events to our database. We then broadcast it to everyone who has that course listed. This way you won't have to scavange Piazza, Blackboard or your email, wondering where that announcement took place.

If you're a TA, these can be your regular office hours, or a one-off review session. Or if you're an instructor, remind your students where and when a midterm takes place.

Alternatively, you can pretty much add whatever course event floats your boat. ScheduleCMU automatically filters out spurious requests to keep everyone's calendars clean.

Your schedule, socialized. (Coming soon)

You share most of your life with your friends online. Why shouldn't you do the same with those 60 units that you've proudly arranged for yourself next semester?

ScheduleCMU integrates with your personal events on Google calendar, so you can see everything in one place. It also lets you share your schedules across the most popular social networking sites. Use ScheduleCMU with:

  • Google Calendar
  • Apple Calendar
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • More to be added...

Now your schedule can have a social life of its own too.
